RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update #79

10th March 2025

Last week saw me attending DIBI in Edinburgh for the Workshop and Conference. It was an amazing couple of days and not only spoke to and connect with so many new people and catch up with a few familiar faces but also learned so much from the workshop and speakers the following day! It was well worth travelling up there and when I came back, I also attended PLATFORM in Sunderland which was one of the most amazing and inspirational events I've been to, so was a fantastic end to an amazing week!

This week started with something interesting that I'll be able to talk about very soon but was a great experience and something I can't wait to see the results of very soon! It will also be good to go to my first Tech on the Tyne this year about The Unconventional routes into technology which should be really interesting!

Next week it will be my birthday so am looking forward to that very much, is great to see me doing more things than ever before, there's no chance of slowing down just yet! It is also great to be able to go along to the North East Tech Quarterly which is an industry forum about the North East's tech ecosystem so am looking forward to that!

RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update #78

3rd March 2025

Last week saw the release of Episode One of my brand-new Podcast called Talking Inspiration where I'll be talking to some of the biggest names in the North East of England starting with Paul Lancaster. Last week also saw the release of the latest Episode of the RoguePlanetoid Podcast about Generative AI & .NET.. It was great to finally see and hear Talking Inspiration and all the hard work that has been put into it to make it what it is and look forward to seeing the rest of the Episodes coming in the next few weeks!

Last week saw a bumper week of events starting with .NET Meetup Newcastle on Tuesday which was followed by another Podcast Paddock from Cluarantonn which features amazing marketing experts talking about things related to podcasting! Thursday saw another amazing Silicon Mingle where I got a chance to catch up with familiar faces and make new connections too and Friday saw Platform - Newcastle organised by Paul Lancaster, which was a great way to end the week, think that was the most events in one week that I've ever done!

This week sees my first conference of the year with DIBI which I've not been to since it moved to Edinburgh, I'm pleased I've got the opportunity to go along and see the amazing speakers including Ayo Owolabi and workshop hosted by Ross Cooney plus it is being hosted by the fantastic Herb Kim the man behind Thinking Digital and TEDx Newcastle. I can't wait to go along and catch up with people there and make new connections there and should be a great way to start off the month of March!
