RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update #22

16th June 2023

This week and the end of last week was in London for the CrimeCon UK True Crime Awards and CrimeCon London as was there helping to represent my wife's Podcast of Scottish Murders. Although it didn't win Best Indie Podcast it was great to be at the awards and also at the conference where I had some cards for the RoguePlanetoid Podcast that surprisingly a few people either knew someone who was interested or was interested themselves which was an unexpected bonus as it was meant to be for True Crime!

I've not worked on anything of my own last week or this week but this weekend I'll be writing the sixth Episode of the RoguePlanetoid Podcast which as mentioned last week will be about Blazor but will also be doing something else next week which I can't wait to talk about once I am able to!

Next week it will be TechNExt which is the Tech Festival for the North East unfortunately many of the events are during the day but am hoping to get along to a couple of them at least. One I will be going to is the one organised by Hainton which should be interesting, there are also some that will be made available online so will be able to catch up with those. These are mostly top-tier events so didn't get involved in any other way but hopefully should something like this happen again, hopefully will be the kind of person who would be able to present or talk at this kind of event in the future!

RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update #21

2nd June 2023

This week saw the release of the latest Episode of the RoguePlanetoid Podcast which was about Microsoft Build 2023. It was a really interesting conference and was good to give my overview of the keynotes and sessions, I also think I imitated the cadence of Panos Panay when talking about his part of the keynote, I found that one really compelling and was a great one to watch but also great to relay as part of the podcast. An additional challenge was I have a cold so didn't quite sound the same as usual along with having to write, record and edit the Episode in just a few day which was much harder to do than expected, but don't expect to do an Episode that close to release again!

This week I will also finishing and recording my talk How sharing can make you a better developer for Scottish Summit which will be released on their YouTube channel closer to the conference. It has been really interesting writing it and used the style of the slides to my advantage, it will have some great points but also some other things too which I hope will make it more interesting, so hoping it might inspire some debate, looking forward to completing and recording it!

The next thing I will be working on will be Blazor for the next Episode of the RoguePlanetoid Podcast and there will also be a companion Article. I'll also be putting together my very first talk on Blazor as have done Workshops but not a dedicated Presentation, also I want to present it at a new event, so if there's a user group out there or online event then let me know on Twitter and I'll see if I can present it there!
