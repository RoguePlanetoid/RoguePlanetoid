Maker Space - 26th July 2023

27th July 2023

This week's visit to MakerSpace this Welcome Wednesday was another chance to use my 3D Printer, I first started by creating a print of Clippy or Clip-it as it was actually known and this printed well. I found a pop-out Space Shuttle that could be printed and made a start on this but while printing I noticed it seemed very thin, although it was to be a card that would be expected but this remained throughout the process. Once the print was completed it was clear that something had gone wrong, the plastic had become bundled up inside the part just before the extruder so no more plastic had come out, which is a known issue with a 3D Printer, unfortunately there wasn't much time to resolve this so that will be my next task when I return in a couple of weeks!

It was great however to see many new people at MakerSpace who were checking it out for the very first time and potentially add more members was really great to see! There are people always working on their projects whether that is using a 3D Printer or the computer-controlled lathe in the basement to precision cut pieces of wood, work on the soldering station to put together an electronic creation or so much more! There's something for everyone at MakerSpace so why not come along next Welcome Wednesday!

Maker Space - 19th July 2023

20th July 2023

It was great to be back to MakerSpace this Welcome Wednesday as had been a few weeks since my last visit as part of the Late Shows! I decided to find a new 3D model of a Zune to print using my 3D Printer and found one that started printing well, but I had made a mistake in not checking the model was 3D printable, it seemed it was a hollow model so when it was sliced it left the inside hollow too, so it printed the bottom perfectly, but by the time it got to the top it tried printing the top in the air above the bottom!

Although printing using my 3D Printer had gone wrong this time it was a great learning experience though and hopefully can get a model of a Zune I can actually print, I was keen to get the one that was mentioned being printed in space to promote Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3! It was also a good chance to catch up with the people there and see what projects they were working on and start planning my next project at MakerSpace, although while waiting for my 3D Printer to finish I started putting together stuff for an up-coming presentation I will be working on, which I'm really looking forward to! It is great to be able to go to MakerSpace and see everyone else there and maybe will see someone new as you can always come along to a future Welcome Wednesday!
