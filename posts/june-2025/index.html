tutorialr.com Workshop Wednesday - Blazor Podcast AI

18th June 2025

It is Workshop Wednesday and tutorialr.com has been updated with a new Workshop for Blazor Podcast AI that you can try!

Blazor Podcast AI

Blazor Podcast AI is a Workshop that shows you how to create a Podcast Assistant using Blazor and GitHub Models to implement an AI Assistant to help with ideas for a Podcast or as the basis of a Custom Assistant. You can also get the Workshop along with the Code for the Workshop on GitHub.
