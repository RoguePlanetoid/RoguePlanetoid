RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update #87

24th November 2025

This month has been a fantastic month where I had the chance to travel down to Manchester to do my Presentation of Take a Note of UI with .NET 10 talk which was whistle-stop tour through the A to (almost) Z of UI technology in .NET, of which I've used them all at some point and some more often that others. I really enjoyed the experience and am hoping to deliver the talk to more groups in the new year, starting with the .NET Meetup - North East in January. It was quite fun to do and one secret is the icons featured for each technology were drawn by me in Photoshop!

Today sees an event I've put together from 6pm to 8pm to celebrate .NET Conf 2025 which is .NET Conf 2025 - Newcastle upon Tyne, this had originally been planned as an in-person event but now is online instead, this did mean I could get more people to speak and attend too so it worked out great in the end and am looking forward to seeing the talk myself and have one on an overview of .NET Conf 2025 and an ask me anything session!

Today also sees the announcement of Comentsys Learning where I'll be offering Courses to help expand or enhance knowledge which will begin with Learning Programming with C# & .NET which will be a six-week live streamed course to join a Cohort of up to twelve or form your own with friends and colleagues. I'm looking forward to delivering this course in 2026 and hope to get as many people as I can started on their journey with C# and .NET, you sign up and get updated and receive a free What is Programming with C# & .NET guide!
