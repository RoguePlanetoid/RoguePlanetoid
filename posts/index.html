RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update #4

3rd January 2023

RoguePlanetoid Podcast launched this week which was hinted at last week, which answered the question of what is the blue for in the RoguePlanetoid logo! The RoguePlanetoid Podcast will be published each month with the first being an Overview of 2022 along with Content Creation with AI. I'm happy to take input from the community to see what should be covered and if possible speak to others in the community and get them on the podcast, if you're interested then can contact me on Twitter.

Hopefully once all the Tutorials for games have been published it might just be the one of the largest collection of free and open-source games using Windows App SDK out there!

There will also be some more Articles being posted here on the website this month which will include a couple about Spotify along with adding some Articles from LinkedIn. It is good updating a website again this year, it is in fact a very special year as it is twenty-five years since I started my original website and there will be Posts, Articles and a Podcast about this coming later in the year!
