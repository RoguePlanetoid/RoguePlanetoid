RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update #53

21st April 2024

This week it was announced officially that I would be speaking at .NET Notts in Nottingham on 28th of April to deliver my talk, Take a Note of XAML with .NET 8! It will be great to deliver this talk again to a new audience both in person and online as will be a hybrid event! If you're able to make it along or what to check it out online then you can sign up for the [HYBRID] ML Algorithms - Simon Painter & XAML with .NET 8 - Peter Bull event which will also feature Simon Painter talking about Basic Machine Learning Algorithms. I'm really looking forward to going along and visiting Nottingham for the very first time, hopefully will have more opportunities to talk at future events around the country!

I'm really pleased I went along and was joined in the queue by so many other people, including one who I chatted along with the whole time which made the queuing not feel so long, is great to be able to support local Record Shops like that and really glad I decided to make the trip and didn't come back empty handed!

Next week I will be getting ready to go along to Crimecon in Glasgow to help represent the Scottish Murders Podcast. We'll have some pens and torches to give away along with stickers for those coming along! I've been to a couple before but wasn't able to make it to the one last year so am really looking forward to being able to go along this year and check out the sessions there too!
