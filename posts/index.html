RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update #42

4th February 2024

This past week there was some good news about the North East Dev Community which was cancelled last month as it is now back on this month on the 27th of February so am looking forward to delivering my talk, Take a Note of XAML with .NET 8, which will cover Windows Presentation Foundation and Windows App SDK and .NET MAUI platforms using XAML with .NET 8. I've also submitted this same topic for DDD North which will be an extended version of the talk going into a bit more detail for each platform.

This past week also saw the latest Episode of the RoguePlanetoid Podcast released about C#, this also marked one year since the launch of the podcast and now has over 280 downloads and appreciate everyone who has listened to the podcast over the past twelve months, it has been a fantastic journey and am pleased so many people have taken the time to listen along with subscribing or following the podcast.

Next week I'll be going along to the Unlocking the value of AI talk where the Innovation SuperNetwork have partnered with Scott Logic and the National Innovation Centre for Data to hold a one-day event about helping organisations in the North East of England understand the potential for harnessing AI for their business development. I've been following Large Language Models since the launch of ChatGPT and use Microsoft Copilot on a regular basis so is great to go along and help support this event and writing an Article about it on this website.
