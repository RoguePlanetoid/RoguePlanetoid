RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update #80

19th May 2025

There's been a lot going on in the background for the past few weeks but now is a good chance to get back to these updates again! Learning has been the main thing with my very first course which I've been planning for a while and will be finalising which is Learning Programming with C# and .NET, I also reached out for some feedback about what to include which will really help shape the course, which will be live online sessions each week going through module to learn the basics of programming for those who may never have done it before or those who may be programming already but haven't tried C# and .NET before, I look forward to sharing more about this very soon including how to sign up for more details where there will be a free part of the course for those who do!

Conferences is the main thing coming up with Microsoft Build this week where I'll be eagerly looking out for sessions online and look forward to hearing from those in Seattle including fellow MVPs, I wish I was there in person but one day I'll make it back there again to Seattle! Next month will be involved in Crimecon helping represent Scottish Murders as well as the True Crime Awards where Cluarantonn is supporting the Best Indie Podcast category. Cluarantonn will also be part of TechNExt where I'll be running a workshop, Build a Podcast AI with Blazor using GitHub Models, as part of the Data and AI Hub which combines my worlds of podcasting and programming together!

Cluarantonn also was proud to be part of the Platform - Edinburgh last week where Dawn and I talked about how we help new and existing podcasters including tips on making the most of appearing on a podcast, it was great to travel up to Scotland for the event and look forward to going back again. Next week we'll be holding our next Podcast Paddock event, past events have been amazing with fantastic feedback from those there, there's not one in June, but we do have the workshop at TechNExt plus another workshop that I'll share details very soon!
