RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update #23

7th July 2023

Is great to post another RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update after a small haitus and getting back into things again with the sixth episode of RoguePlanetoid Podcast which was about Blazor! I actually wrote, recorded, edited and posted the Episode just a day before release, something I don't plan on doing again, which is ironic as the previous Episode about Microsoft Build 2023 I thought was done at short notice but was actually completed earlier!

This week was Thinking Digital which comprised of the 10th Annual Start-up Competition and the Conference itself and can read those Articles about. It was a real challenge writing those and getting them posted the same day, was amazing to see the Article about the Start-up competition featured in Newcastle Tech Digest! Thinking Digital was fantastic, and it was great to speak to the people I met both days, who I hope to see again, along with the amazing start-ups and speakers, both of which were interesting to hear from. I'm glad I decided to go along in person, and hopefully will make it along to next year!

Some announcements of some upcoming appearances that I've not mentioned anywhere else, so you'll hear about it here first well before I announce it elsewhere! The first is I will be on The .NET Core Podcast with Jamie Taylor who is an amazing developer and content creator, it was fantastic to be able to be a guest and can't wait to hear it when it is published! The second is I will be on Smart Cherrys Thoughts by Sai Charan Paloju who was an amazing interviewer and asked me really great questions with hopefully really great answers! I'm hoping these opportunities will be the first of many more appearances on podcasts or videos by others!
