RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update #76

24th November 2024

Last week it was great to see .NET Conf 2024,, although I was ill during it so wasn't able to enjoy much of the content at the time, and also turned out to be a good thing I didn't have a talk to do for it after all, it was great to see the release of .NET 9 along with other announcements such as Hot Design from Uno Platform, which is actually one of the projects I'd been hinting at recently and will be writing an Article about soon!

Last week was also a chance to organise my very first developer event here in the North East of England with .NET Conf 2024 - Newcastle upon Tyne which will be on 4th December 6pm to 8pm with lightning talks from members of North East Dev Community along with myself where I'll be talking about .NET Conf 2024 along with a preview of Hot Design in Uno Platform, I'm looking forward to seeing the other talks in the evening, and was also grateful of the support of Mike Dent from Waterstons who will be providing the pizza on the night!

Next week will see the second Podcast Paddock event from Cluarantonn which will be a chance for those there to hear from local studios to chatting to me about what they have to offer and so much more so if you've not yet signed up then you can get your ticket for the event this Wednesday between 7pm and 9pm now!

RoguePlanetoid Weekly Update #75

10th November 2024

Last week there were so many things to get ready for but was great to get the latest Episode of the RoguePlanetoid Podcast released about ASP.NET Core and as always appreciate those who have taken the time to listen to the podcast, it's a few hours work for just a few minutes but it is worth it when I see people downloading the Podcast and hopefully finding it interesting and informative plus this month will see the release of .NET 9 which will be the topic for next month's Episode of the RoguePlanetoid Podcast!

Last week it was good to start planning things for next year already with the first few months of the year already having at least one thing I'm going to be doing planned which is great! It has also been amazing to record another Episode of Talking Inspiration which is my upcoming Podcast which has reached the halfway point of recording series one, it has been amazing to speak to those who've kindly agreed to take part and looking forward to listening to the finished result when it is released.

This week I'll be attending North East Expo where Cluarantonn will be exhibiting to give those there an opportunity to find out how to get started or get support with podcasting along with how small businesses and freelancers can get help too or connect those offering products and services to podcasters. It will also be .NET Conf 2024 this week which will see the announcement of .NET 9 and although I'm not presenting this time it will be great to catch as many sessions as possible!
