Mike Oldfield

25th June 2023

CESPage.com 25th Anniversary
Earlier this year I wrote about Tubular Bells 50th Anniversary which is marked this year in 2023 when the retirement of Mike Oldfield was announced, but this year also marks another Anniversary with the 25h Anniversary of the predecessor of RoguePlanetoid.com which was CESPage.com that started back in 1998 but a few years later in 2005 I launched a dedicated website to Mike Oldfield which included a Discography which is reproduced here along with Embeds from Spotify where possible and a couple of albums after that section of the website closed to help celebrate fifty years of Tubular Bells and twenty-five years of CESPage.com.
