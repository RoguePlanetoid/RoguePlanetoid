Zune - Welcome, guardians

1st May 2023

Zune.net has been relaunched as Microsoft, Redwire, the International Space Station National Laboratory, and NASA embrace diversity and want to reinforce an inclusive culture to help achieve our goals in space-based exploration, on earth, the galaxy and beyond. In Marvel Studios Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3, the Guardians are made up of individuals from different backgrounds, species, and planets, each with their unique set of skills and perspectives. It's precisely this diversity that enables them to overcome seemingly insurmountable challenges and save the galaxy.

Redwire shows you how 3D printing works in space by going behind the scenes with their engineers and experts to learn more about the design process and nuances that come with 3D printing in environments such as the International Space Station and the impact it will have towards the continuous presence of humans in space. There are teaching resources with classroom activities, or you can code an experience to guide the Guardians' new ship through space to update damage satellites or you can join the Zune prototyping crew and create a music player using Make Code Arcade.

You can become a space developer and build tech skills for space exploration including exploring modules and learning paths inspired by NASA scientists to prepare for a # career in space exploration and even lean about some Microsoft space developers as they discuss their career journey. You can also find out more about Zune, which is still a fantastic media player in 2023, even in the far reaches of the galaxy! However, getting Zune running can be a challenge on modern operating systems so Scott Hanselman from Microsoft helps guide you through the process of reviving a Microsoft Zune in 2023 and even loads some Marvel Studios' Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 footage onto his collection of Zunes.

If you want to find out more about the Zune from Microsoft then why not check out Zunepedia, which is a free digital coffee table book of an unofficial journey of the history of the Zune devices, hardware, services and the community that keeps it alive to this day. You can find out all about the original Zune that is featured in "Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3" along with the later Zune software, hardware and more.
