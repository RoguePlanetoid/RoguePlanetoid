Xbox 360

25th September 2023

CESPage 25th Anniversary
Xbox 360 was the biggest console launch I had seen where I queued up and picked up one in person. Xbox 360 was covered a lot on the Xbox section of CESPage from before launch until before the end of the console, with resources I put together being used to help sell the consoles by a store locally, without knowing it was someone local who had created them! To celebrate the peak of popularity of the Xbox section along with twenty-five years of CESPage here is information about the Xbox 360 available at launch and Xbox 360 Arcade, Xbox 360 Elite along with the Xbox 360 S which rebooted the branding and design. Of course Xbox is still around today with the Xbox Series S and Xbox Series X and you can find out more about them at Xbox.com!

Xbox 360

Xbox 360 video game and entertainment system placed you at the centre of the experience. Xbox 360 ignited a new era of digital entertainment that is always connected, always personalised, and always in high definition, you can see some of the features below:

  • Graphics - Xbox 360 redefines what games look like, sound like, feel like, and play like to engage you like never before. With Xbox 360, epic worlds are alive with detail, from thunderous skies rumbling over a mountain range to tiny blades of grass rustling together in the breeze. All games support 720p resolution in 16:9 widescreen, with anti-aliasing for smooth, movie-like graphics and multi-channel surround sound.
  • Digital Entertainment - Xbox 360 allows you to do more with your music, photos, video, and TV. Watch progressive-scan DVD movies right out of the box. Copy music to the Xbox 360 hard drive and share your latest digital pictures with friends. Xbox 360 can stream digital media stored on your MP3 player, digital camera, Media Center PC, or any Windows XP based PC.
  • Personalise - Xbox 360 lets you create your own unique system and experience, with interchangeable faces it's easy and fun to change the appearance of your console. Switch on your system and customise the look and feel of the Xbox Gamer Guide and Xbox System Guide.
  • Xbox Live - Xbox 360 when connected to your broadband connection gives you instant access to Xbox Live Silver. Talk with others using voice chat and access Xbox Live Marketplace-all right out of the box at no extra cost. Upgrade to Xbox Live Gold with intelligent matchmaking, access to achievements and statistics and video chat and messaging.

Xbox 360 Core

Xbox 360 Core gave buyers a choice at launch with a cheaper console which lacked the chrome accent on the disc drive along with excluding the hard-drive, you can see what was included below:

  • Xbox 360 Console - Powerful and sleek high-definition gaming and entertainment
  • Wired Controller - Precision, Control, Comfort
  • Xbox Live - Includes free Xbox Live Silver, 30-Day trial of Xbox Live Gold Included (full Gold version sold separately)
  • Ethernet Connectivity cable - A fast, direct connection
  • Composite AV Cable - Superior audio/video performance for standard TVs

Xbox 360 Arcade

Xbox 360 Arcade console was the first Xbox 360 console to include five family-friendly games, a wireless controller, a high-definition multimedia interface (HDMI) connection to enable high-definition output if desired and 256 MB of memory useful for storing games and entertainment content.

  • Xbox 360 Console - Three powerful core processors capable of producing the best in HD entertainment up to 1080p, 16:9 cinematic aspect ratio, anti-aliasing for smooth textures, full surround sound and DVD playback.
  • 256MB Memory Unit - 256 MB of memory allows gamers to save their games and other content available from Xbox LIVE Marketplace.
  • Wireless Controller - Precision, comfort, and control with wireless freedom.
  • Xbox Live - Includes free Xbox Live Silver, 30-Day trial of Xbox Live Gold Included (full Gold version sold separately)
  • Standard AV Cable - Optimised for standard televisions.
  • Xbox 360 Arcade Disc - Includes five full Xbox Live Arcade games including PAC-MAN Championship Edition, Uno, Luxor 2, Boom Boom Rocket and Feeding Frenzy plus trial games, videos and more

Xbox 360 Elite

Xbox 360 Elite, was a new model of the video game and entertainment system that included a 120GB hard drive, a high-definition multimedia interface (HDMI) port, a high-definition cable, and a premium black finish for the console, wireless controller and Xbox LIVE headset, you can see some of the features below:

  • Xbox 360 Elite Console - Equipped with a premium black finish, three powerful core processors capable of producing the best in HD entertainment up to 1080p, 16:9 cinematic aspect ratio, anti-aliasing for smooth textures, full surround sound, HDMI output and DVD playback with upscaling capabilities right out of the box.
  • 120GB Hard Drive - The 120GB detachable hard drive allows gamers to save their games and store television shows, movies, music, pictures, trailers, levels, demos and other content available from Xbox LIVE Marketplace. Black Wireless Controller - Precision, comfort, and control with wireless freedom.
  • Xbox Live - Includes free Xbox Live Silver, 30-Day trial of Xbox Live Gold Included (full Gold version sold separately)
  • Black Headset - Talk, laugh, and listen on Xbox Live with this headset featuring a black finish to match the console
  • Ethernet Connectivity cable - A fast, direct connection
  • HDMI Cable - New to Xbox 360, HDMI allows consumers to get HD video (up to 1080p) and multichannel surround sound, all from one cable, plus additional cable for stereo and optical output for 5.1 surround sound.
  • Component HDAV Cable - Optimised for standard and HDTVs

Xbox 360 S

Xbox 360 250 GB video game and entertainment system was back in black, with a sleek and lean look. Quieter than ever, this Xbox 360 came with built-in Wi-Fi, a 250 GB hard drive and is designed for the easiest connection to controller-free fun with the Xbox 360 Kinect, you can see some of the features below:

  • Xbox 360 Console - A brand new, leaner machine in an all-new black gloss finish.
  • 250 GB Hard Drive - Includes removable internal 250 GB HDD - more than double the space for all your entertainment.
  • Xbox 360 Wireless Controller - Xbox 360 Wireless Controller features an updated look in all-black with colour-matched Thumbsticks and D-pad for a seamless look with a touch of chrome on the guide button.
  • Xbox LIVE - Xbox LIVE brings a whole world of entertainment possibilities directly to your Xbox 360. Play with friends online and watch streaming films and TV from Zune in crystal-clear HD.
  • Xbox 360 Headset - Talk it up with friends and family around the world.
  • Composite AV Standard Definition cable - Connect to your TV for the Xbox 360 experience.
